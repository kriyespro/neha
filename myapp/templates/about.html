How We Started

From a Kitchen Table to a Beloved Brand

VibrantGlaze began in 2023 when Neha, a resin art enthusiast from Surat, Gujarat, started experimenting with pressed flower preservation in her home studio. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a small business when friends and family fell in love with her signature "gloss-glazed" botanical creations.

blockquote>"I wanted each piece to carry a story — a memory, a letter, a flower that reminded someone of a special moment."
— Neha, Founder

Today, VibrantGlaze ships handcrafted resin art and premium imitation jewellery across India, serving over 500 customers who trust us with their most personal milestones — weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, and everyday elegance.

What We Stand For

The principles behind every piece we create

Artisan Craftsmanship

Every piece is individually handcrafted — no mass production, no shortcuts. Each pour is a unique expression of care and creativity.

Natural Ingredients

We use locally sourced dried botanicals — real flowers, sea sand, and natural minerals — preserved sustainably within our resin.

Safe & Skin-Friendly

All jewellery uses lead-free, nickel-free brass. Our epoxy resin is fully cured, BPA-free and non-toxic after hardening.

Customer First

From flexible customizations to rapid WhatsApp support, we ensure every customer interaction is warm, personal, and memorable.

Thoughtful Gifting

Every order arrives in premium gift packaging with a handwritten personal note — making every unboxing a special moment.

Uncompromising Quality

Multi-grit sanding, UV-stabilized coating, and quality control inspection ensure every piece meets our glass-glaze standard.

Meet the Maker

The Heart Behind VibrantGlaze

Neha

Founder & Head Artisan

Based out of Godadara, Surat, Gujarat, Neha is a self-taught resin artist who discovered her passion for botanical preservation and custom jewellery creation in 2023. Inspired by the idea that handcrafted gifts carry the most emotion, she launched VibrantGlaze to help people gift personalised, lasting treasures rather than generic products.

When she's not pouring resin or curating floral embeddings, she's exploring new material combinations — amethyst pigments, ocean sand, gold leaf — to keep every VibrantGlaze creation fresh and distinctive.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped VibrantGlaze

2023 — Early
Neha begins experimenting with resin art at home, preserving dried flowers in epoxy for personal use. First Instagram post goes viral with 2,000+ likes overnight.
2023 — Mid
First 50 orders fulfilled entirely through WhatsApp and Google Pay. The signature "A-Z Initial Keychain" becomes the first bestseller.
2024 — Jan
VibrantGlaze.com launches as a full e-commerce website. Imitation jewellery category added. First bulk corporate gifting order — 80 keychains for a Surat textile company.
2024 — Mid
500th customer milestone reached. Royal Kundan Bridal Set launched, becoming the highest-rated product at a perfect 5.0 stars.
2025 — Present
Fully revamped digital storefront with custom admin dashboard. Shipping pan-India across all 28 states. Actively exploring wholesale partnerships.
