From Pour to Polish

The 6-step artisanal process behind every VibrantGlaze piece

1

Conceptualise

Client brief, mood board, color palette selection and material sourcing.

2

Mould Preparation

Silicone mould selection, release coat application and botanical/crystal embedding.

3

Resin Pour

Epoxy mixing at precise ratios, pigment blending, layer-by-layer pours for depth.

4

Curing & Demoulding

24–48 hour UV-assisted cure cycle, followed by careful piece release.

5

Sanding & Polishing

Multi-grit wet sanding (220→3000 grit) to achieve a crystal-clear glass glaze.

6

Packaging & Dispatch

Luxury gift box wrap with a handwritten note, care guide and bubble protection.
