VibrantGlaze product images
============================

Place all storefront product and brand images in this folder.

Expected filenames (referenced by the catalog):
- logo.jpg
- hero-new.jpg (homepage hero background)
- resin_keychain.png
- resin_pendant.png
- pearl_earrings.png
- kundan_necklace.png
- earring_ruby_teardrop.png
- earring_ruby_square.png
- earring_blue_daisy.png
- earring_skyblue_daisy.png
- earring_teal_daisy.png

Uploaded admin product images are saved here as product_<uuid>.<ext>

Flask serves these at: /static/images/<filename>
Legacy fallback also works at: /<filename>
